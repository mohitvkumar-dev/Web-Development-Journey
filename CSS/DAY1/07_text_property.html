Iron Man

Find Avengers Iron Man is one of the most famous superheroes in the Marvel Universe.
His real name is Tony Stark.
He is a billionaire, genius inventor, and the owner of Stark Industries.
Using his intelligence and technology, he created a powerful armored suit called the Iron Man suit.

Origin of Iron Man

Tony Stark was captured by terrorists while demonstrating weapons.
During captivity, he built a small arc reactor to keep himself alive.
Using his engineering skills, he secretly created the first Iron Man armor.
With this suit, he escaped and later improved the technology.

Powers and Abilities

The Iron Man suit gives Tony Stark many powerful abilities.
It allows him to fly using jet propulsion systems.
The suit also has advanced weapons, missiles, and laser beams.
Artificial intelligence systems like JARVIS help him control the suit.

Iron Man in Avengers

Iron Man is a founding member of the Avengers team.
He fights alongside heroes like Captain America, Thor, and Hulk.
His technology and leadership play an important role in protecting Earth.
Iron Man sacrifices himself to defeat Thanos and save the universe.
